

Grey: roads
	grey/green: road anchors (top left tile)
	grey/purple: top left of future intersection
	grey/brown: other corners of future intersection
	grey/blue: top left of 3-way intersection (rotated dynamically while drawing, not by flags)
	grey/white: other corners of 3-way intersection

Yellow: controller
	yellow/red: unloader flags
	yellow/green: carrier flags

Orange: source
	orange/red: loader flags
	orange/green: carrier flags

Blue: construction
	blue/blue: heavy builder flag
	blue/green: carrier flags (to feed heavy builders)

Cyan: future construction sites
	cyan/orange: extension
	cyan/yellow: spawn
